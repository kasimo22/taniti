Top Attractions in Taniti

Whether you're looking for adventure or relaxation, Taniti has something for everyone.

Beaches

Enjoy the pristine sandy and rocky shores along Yellow Leaf Bay.

Rainforest

Immerse yourself in the lush, vibrant beauty of Taniti’s tropical rainforest.

Active Volcano

Take a thrilling visit to Taniti’s active volcano and witness its natural power.

History Museum

Discover the rich history and culture of Taniti at the local history museum.

Fishing Tours

Join a chartered fishing tour and experience the best fishing spots around the island.

Zip-Lining

Zip-line through the rainforest for an exhilarating aerial view of Taniti’s landscape.
