Transportation Options

From arrival to local transport, Taniti has convenient options for every traveler.

Airport

Taniti is accessible by air through its small airport, with plans for future expansion to accommodate larger jets.

Cruise Ship

Taniti welcomes a small cruise ship weekly, offering passengers a one-night stay to explore the island.

Public Buses

Public buses serve Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily, providing an affordable way to get around.

Taxis

Available throughout Taniti City, taxis offer a convenient way to travel around the island.

Car Rentals

Rent a car from a local agency near the airport to explore Taniti at your own pace.

Bike Rentals

Rent bikes and helmets from several vendors for an eco-friendly way to explore Taniti City and nearby areas.
